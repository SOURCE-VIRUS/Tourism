ABOUT OWERRI TOURISM

Welcome to Owerri Tourism, your ultimate guide to the beautiful city of Owerri, Nigeria. We are dedicated to showcasing the diverse cultural heritage and natural wonders that Owerri has to offer. Whether you are a local resident or a curious traveler, we aim to provide you with the best information to make your visit to Owerri memorable.

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism in Owerri while preserving its rich history and culture. We strive to support local businesses and create opportunities for the community to thrive through responsible tourism practices.


Explore the enchanting attractions, indulge in the local delicacies, and immerse yourself in the vibrant traditions of Owerri. Let us be your guide to the hidden gems and iconic landmarks that make Owerri a truly unique destination.


Join us in our journey to discover the heart of Owerri and create lasting memories in this captivating city.
